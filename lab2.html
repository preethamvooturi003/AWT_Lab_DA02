Idly

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Idly Rice
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak idly rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter separately. Add water as needed.
  3. Mix both batters together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.
  4. Pour the batter into idly molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Vada

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Urad Dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Oil, for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-6 hours and drain.
  2. Grind dal with cumin seeds, black pepper, and salt, adding minimal water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, shape the batter into small flat rounds, and deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney.

Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Dosa Rice
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak dosa rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter separately. Add salt.
  3. Mix both batters together and let it ferment overnight.
  4. Heat a dosa griddle, pour batter, spread into a thin circle, and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Coconut, grated
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Peanuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon Tamarind Paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Grind coconut, green chilies, roasted peanuts, tamarind paste, and salt with water into a smooth paste.
  2. Adjust the consistency with water as needed.
  3. Serve with idly, dosa, or vada.
